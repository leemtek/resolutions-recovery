The Sober Living Experience

Resolutions Recovery is more than a sober living environment we are a sober living experience.

What sets us apart from other SLE’s is the high quality of service and customized level of care we provide to each of our clients. With significant experience in addiction and recovery, our staff is well equipped to guide and support our clients in their journey to independence, self-discovery and a successful design for living.

We know first-hand the challenges that face those overcoming addiction and are here with you every step of the way. Our main goal is to provide a safe and comfortable living environment where you can acquire the tools necessary to make your recovery and new life the most successful it can be. We provide the resources that you need, you put them into action to succeed.

A Safe + Supportive Recovery Environment

Resolutions Recovery sober living is committed to providing its clients with a safe, sober and supportive environment. A clean and positive living experience is critical to one’s recovery. With two properties located in the Marin area, we help facilitate a new way of living without the use of drugs or alcohol.

“Resolutions Recovery creates a warm, supportive environment while providing the necessary structure to foster change”- L Exstein

Guided by the 12-Step Principles of Alcoholics Anonymous, along with our ‘Democratic House Structure,’ we cultivate both personal and spiritual growth in each of our residents. We welcome those who sincerely wish to take action in their sobriety and recovery. We help our clients learn, develop and implement new living skills that will provide them with a lasting recovery and an abundant life.

Services + Groups

Our customized service include:

  • A zero-tolerance, drug + alcohol free environment
  • Random multi-panel alcohol and drug testing
  • 12-step involvement with sponsoring
  • Morning group meeting for goal setting + accountability
  • Groups for education, support and life-skills training
  • Nutrition + healthful living
  • Service or volunteerism
  • Case management

Support groups that Resolutions Recovery provide our clients at our office location include:

  • Gender specific process groups
  • Relapse prevention
  • Meditation group
  • Weekly hike

Twelve Step Meetings

We believe that 12 step programs guide those in recovery to spiritual development and provide daily practices that lead to healthy, sober living. However, we do understand that the 12 step specific approach is not for everyone, so we also support additional recovery platforms.

Our staff can help guide and assist with various types of programs and details of them.

CASE MANAGEMENT

Clients of Resolutions Recovery sober living residences receive customized weekly meetings with a drug and alcohol counselor at our office location.

We also offer case management services to individuals residing outside RR homes.

Services may include:

  • Weekly or biweekly meetings
  • Drug and alcohol testing
  • Learning to budget
  • Time management skills
  • Collaboration with treatment team
