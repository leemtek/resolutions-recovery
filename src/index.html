The Sober Living Experience

Resolutions Recovery is more than a sober living environment we are a sober living experience.

What sets us apart from other SLE’s is the high quality of service and customized level of care we provide to each of our clients. With significant experience in addiction and recovery, our staff is well equipped to guide and support our clients in their journey to independence, self-discovery and a successful design for living.

We know first-hand the challenges that face those overcoming addiction and are here with you every step of the way. Our main goal is to provide a safe and comfortable living environment where you can acquire the tools necessary to make your recovery and new life the most successful it can be. We provide the resources that you need, you put them into action to succeed.

Call for more information or to set up an appointment to visit one of our properties. (925) 727-5774

Let Resolutions Recovery help you achieve a lifetime of sustainable recovery.

Services + Groups

Our customized service include:

  • A zero-tolerance, drug + alcohol free environment
  • Random multi-panel alcohol and drug testing
  • 12-step involvement with sponsoring
  • Morning group meeting for goal setting + accountability
  • Groups for education, support and life-skills training
  • Nutrition + healthful living
  • Service or volunteerism
  • Case management

Support groups that Resolutions Recovery provide our clients at our office location include:

  • Gender specific process groups
  • Relapse prevention
  • Meditation group
  • Weekly hike
